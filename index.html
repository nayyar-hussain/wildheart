✦ The Studio Process

How Each Piece is Born

01

Concept & Vision

Every painting begins with a feeling — a moment, a memory, or an emotion that demands to be captured on canvas.

02

Sketching & Composition

Countless studies and sketches refine the composition until every element sings in perfect harmony.

03

Layers of Color

Paint is applied in deliberate layers — building texture, depth, and luminosity over days or weeks of work.

04

Final Refinement

The piece rests, then returns for final touches — the last brushstrokes that bring everything to life.

✦ The Artist

Wild He Art

Fine Artist · Zurich, Switzerland

Born and raised in Zurich, Wild He Art discovered his love for painting at age seven, watching sunlight dance through the studio window of his grandfather's apartment. What began as childhood wonder evolved into a lifelong devotion to capturing the ineffable beauty of the human experience on canvas.

After studying at the School of Visual Arts in Zurich and training under masters in Florence, Italy, WildHeArt developed a signature style that blends the luminous technique of the Old Masters with the raw emotional intensity of contemporary expressionism.

His work has been exhibited in galleries across Zurich, London, Dubai, and Tokyo — and collected by art lovers in over 92 countries. Each painting is a window into a world where color and emotion are one.
